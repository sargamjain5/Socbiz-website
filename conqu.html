About Conquest

Conquest is the flagship consulting competition organized by our club, designed to simulate real-world problem-solving scenarios and provide a platform for students to showcase their strategic thinking and analytical prowess.

The event kicks off with the release of a challenging, real-world business problem statement, carefully crafted to reflect the complexity and depth of actual consulting assignments. Participants are required to dive deep into research, structure their thoughts, and propose innovative, data-driven solutions.
