Empowering Global Connections, One Delivery at a Time

Tvelp is a P2P international delivery platform connecting shoppers and international travellers.

The Challenge

Tvelp's primary challenge is attracting and retaining registered travellers, which is the supply side of its business. Despite consistent demand from shoppers, the platform needs to reinforce its traveller base. Furthermore, Tvelp aims to increase brand awareness and build trust among its users to ensure a positive and reliable experience for everyone involved. The startup has sought assistance from the Society of Business IIT Roorkee to address these critical issues.

Our Solutions

Express Delivery with Cabin Crew

Introducing an "Express Delivery" option by partnering with airline cabin crew to provide swift cross-border delivery, incentivized with an enhanced commission.

Enhanced Trust and Transparency

Adding features like a delivery counter for travellers, an "Upload Bill" function to prevent price discrepancies, and a dedicated page explaining the legality of the process.

Strategic Marketing & Partnerships

Focusing on educational content for social media and exploring partnerships with travel agencies and e-commerce platforms to expand reach.

Cataloging of Indian Products

Launching a user-generated content movement to catalogue unique Indian goods, helping US shoppers discover more products on the platform.

Conclusion

The strategies outlined here are designed to address Tvelp's key challenges of attracting travellers and building user trust. By focusing on innovative solutions like the Express Delivery service and implementing new features to ensure transparency and legality, Tvelp aims to strengthen its platform. The proposed marketing, partnership, and cataloging initiatives will work in tandem to increase brand recognition and foster a reliable, community-driven service for both shoppers and travellers.
