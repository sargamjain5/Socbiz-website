Who are WE?

A dynamic student-led organization dedicated to nurturing future leaders in consulting, product management, and data analytics. Established in 2009 and rebranded in 2023, SocBiz has consistently provided a platform for students to engage in real-world business challenges and develop essential skills.

OUR VERTICALS

Through structured curricula, case competitions, and real-life projects, students learn to apply product management frameworks and tools. For instance, members have worked on projects like minimizing e-commerce returns and improving user onboarding for startups.
This vertical aims to prepare students for data-driven decision-making roles by providing hands-on experience in analyzing and deriving insights from data.
Through these activities, students gain insights into the consulting industry and prepare for careers in management consulting.

PROJECTS

We collaborate with startups, established companies, and non-profits to solve real-world problems through structured, data-driven consulting engagements.
