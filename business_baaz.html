About Business Baazigar

Business Baazigar is the flagship product management challenge hosted by the Society of Business, IIT Roorkee. This week-long competition transforms first-year students into strategic thinkers through real-life product scenarios, innovative ideation, and professional pitching.

The event begins with the release of a compelling product challenge that mirrors real-world scenarios faced by leading tech companies and startups. Participants dive deep into user research, problem definition, solution ideation, and prototype development.

Through interactive rounds and mentorship-driven feedback, participants explore user research methodologies, problem-solving frameworks, wireframing techniques, and professional pitching—all condensed into one transformative experience that builds foundational PM skills.

Evaluation Parameters

Problem Understanding

Demonstrate deep comprehension of user pain points and ability to define the right problem to solve

Solution Innovation

Present creative, feasible solutions that are perfectly aligned with identified user needs and market gaps

Strategic Thinking

Showcase structured PM mindset through MVP definition, trade-off analysis, and strategic decision-making

Presentation Excellence

Deliver compelling pitches with clear articulation, engaging visuals, and persuasive storytelling
